Purpose of the Health Equity Department

The purpose of this department is to ensure all Kern Family Health Care members (over 400,000 Medi‑Cal enrollees, about 90% of Kern’s Medi‑Cal population) can access high‑quality care, no matter their income, race/ethnicity, or where they live.
